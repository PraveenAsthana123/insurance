## The 4P framework (mandatory sections per §64.4)

### P1 — People

- Org-chart impact: roles created / collapsed / re-tooled
- Change-mgmt risk: low / med / high — and mitigation
- Training plan: who needs what, by when
- Cultural shift: from … to …
- Resistance points + counter-narratives

### P2 — Process

- TO-BE process maps (link to `HOLY_FLOW.md`)
- Automation % target per process (current vs goal)
- Decision authority shift: where AI auto-decides + where HITL stays
- New process cadence (real-time / batch / event-driven)
- KPIs feeding the TO-BE measurement

### P3 — Profit

- Cost-out: $ saved per year, by category (labor, infra, error-cost)
- Revenue-up: $ gained per year, by source (new product, faster cycle, better targeting)
- ROI horizon: 6 / 12 / 36 months — per initiative
- Risk-adjusted NPV
- Sensitivity analysis (best / base / worst case)

### P4 — Technology

- Stack shift (from ↔ to) per layer (data / model / orchestration / UI)
- Build vs buy decisions per capability
- Vendor lock-in risk
- Technical-debt impact (paying down vs accruing)
- Migration sequencing (strangler-fig path)
